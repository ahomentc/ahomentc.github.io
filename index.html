Suggested Story Details:

~ The year is 2051 and the world has moved to Ethereum and Virtual Reality.

~ NFTs give abilities in the virtual world. They've become the center of culture and work.

~ Satoshi Nakamoto reappears with a cryptic message: Find my keys, with 4 NFTs. 1 punk, 1 ape, 1 loot, 1 cat.

~ A early 2020s NFT researcher sees this and embarks on a quest to find them.

~ His journey will take him through the different NFT virtual words that exist in 2051 as well as the history of early NFTs.

To keep the story together, only 1 person can mint at a time. Get the current minting access code from the last person to mint or wait for it to expire (we'll tweet when it does). DM us if you lost your code or didn't recieve one.
